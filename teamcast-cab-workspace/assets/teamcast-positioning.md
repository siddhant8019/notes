# Teamcast Positioning

---

## One-Liner
Teamcast helps hiring teams reduce resume noise and deliver interview-ready shortlists using structured AI-led assessments.

## Core Value Proposition
Instead of manual screening, Teamcast evaluates skills, communication, and role fit before recruiters spend time on early calls. The result: fully vetted, interview-ready Top 3 candidates within 72 hours.

## What Teamcast Replaces
- Manual resume screening
- Inconsistent recruiter evaluations
- Time spent on unqualified early-stage calls
- Resume-driven hiring decisions

## What Teamcast Delivers
- Structured AI interviews that assess real skills
- Objective candidate scoring (not gut-feel)
- Interview-ready shortlists, not raw lead lists
- Consistency across hiring teams and geographies
- Speed: Top 3 candidates in 72 hours

## Positioning vs Competitors
- **Not a generic ATS**: Teamcast is an evidence-based screening engine
- **Not a sourcing tool**: Teamcast evaluates candidates already in the pipeline
- **Not a chatbot**: Teamcast runs structured assessments, not Q&A
- **Not a resume parser**: Teamcast evaluates skills, communication, and role fit directly

## Key Phrases for Outreach
Use these when describing Teamcast in CAB emails:
- "structured AI-led assessments"
- "interview-ready shortlists"
- "evidence-based hiring"
- "objective candidate scoring"
- "reduce resume noise"
- "recruiter bandwidth"
- "hiring efficiency"

## Phrases to Avoid
- "AI-powered hiring platform" (too generic)
- "Recruitment automation" (sounds like a bot)
- "Talent intelligence" (vague)
- "HR tech solution" (commodity language)
