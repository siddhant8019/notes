## Why This ICP

### Pain Points We Solve
- **High applicant volume**: Too many resumes, not enough time to screen properly
- **Inconsistent screening**: Different recruiters evaluate differently
- **Recruiter bandwidth**: Skilled recruiters spending time on unqualified candidates
- **Speed to hire**: Competitive market demands faster, more confident decisions

### Why These Companies
- **0-500 headcount**: Process maturity is incomplete, pain is acute, willingness to experiment is higher
- **Recently funded**: Hiring pain is imminent, budget exists for solutions
- **15%+ growth**: Already scaling and likely feeling recruiter bandwidth pressure
- **Tech/SaaS**: More receptive to AI-led solutions, faster decision cycles

### Why These Titles
- **TA/Recruiting leaders**: Direct users, feel the screening pain daily
- **People/HR leaders**: Strategic buyers, care about process consistency and defensibility
- **C-suite (secondary)**: Economic buyers, care about hiring velocity and cost efficiency
