# Campaign Brief — Teamcast Customer Advisory Board

---

## Campaign Name
Teamcast CAB Outreach

## Campaign Type
Customer Advisory Board (CAB) invitation — soft-entry sales motion

## Objective
Engage senior Talent Acquisition and People leaders at scaling, tech-forward companies. Position Teamcast as the AI-powered engine that delivers fully vetted, interview-ready Top 3 candidates within 72 hours. Book short introductory conversations through CAB invitation framing.

## Why CAB, Not Direct Sales
- CAB flips the power dynamic: "we want your brain" vs "we want your money"
- Prospects feel elevated as experts, not targeted as buyers
- Lower perceived risk leads to higher response rates
- Creates advocates who provide product insight AND become strategic pipeline
- Early-stage companies benefit most from this approach — no brand recognition needed

## What We Offer CAB Members
- Direct input to Teamcast product roadmap
- Early access to product features
- Private benchmarking insights
- Peer networking with other hiring/recruiting leaders

## What We're Really Doing
- Building strategic relationships with ideal buyers
- Getting product feedback from market experts
- Creating a pipeline of warm prospects who understand Teamcast deeply
- Generating referrals from senior leaders

## Success Metrics
- Positive reply rate > 5%
- Meetings booked per week
- CAB conversations that lead to product insight
- Referral rate from outreach
- Product-interest conversion after CAB conversation
