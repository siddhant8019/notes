## Action Key
- **routed_to_utkarsh**: Positive reply or referral sent to Utkarsh
- **suppressed**: Contact added to suppression list
- **deferred**: Re-engagement scheduled for later date
- **escalated**: Sent to human operator for review
- **no_action**: Auto-reply noted, no action needed
