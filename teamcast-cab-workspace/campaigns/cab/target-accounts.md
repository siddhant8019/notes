# Target Accounts

> Companies identified as CAB campaign targets. Updated by the Sourcing Agent.

---

## Format

| Company | Domain | Industry | Headcount | Funding | Growth % | Geo | Fit Score | Status | Batch |
|---------|--------|----------|-----------|---------|----------|-----|-----------|--------|-------|
| [pending] | | | | | | | | | |

---

## Status Key
- **sourced**: Found by Sourcing Agent, pending contact search
- **contacts_found**: Contacts identified at this company
- **researched**: Signals extracted for contacts at this company
- **in_progress**: Emails drafted or sent to contacts at this company
- **completed**: All contacts at this company have been processed
- **excluded**: Company excluded (reason logged)

---

## Notes
- Max 2 contacts per company per batch
- Companies with fit score < 6 are excluded
- Check against suppression list (domain-level) before adding
