---

## Status Key
- **sourced**: Found by Sourcing Agent
- **researched**: Signal extracted by Research Agent
- **drafted**: Email drafted by Messaging Agent
- **qa_approved**: Passed QA review
- **qa_rejected**: Failed QA review (see blocked-items)
- **sent**: Email sent
- **replied**: Reply received (see replies-log)
- **suppressed**: Added to suppression list
- **deferred**: Prospect asked to be contacted later

---

## Persona Key
- **ta_leader**: Head/Director/VP of Talent Acquisition
- **recruiting_leader**: Director/VP of Recruiting
- **people_leader**: CPO, VP People, Head of People
- **recruiting_ops**: Talent Ops, Recruiting Ops
- **individual_recruiter**: Senior/Lead/Technical Recruiter
- **c_suite**: CEO, COO, CFO
- **hr_leader**: VP HR, Director People Ops
