# Email 03 — Alternate Initial Email

> Use when: Signal is more operational/scaling-based, target is recruiting ops / TA management, "high growth teams" framing fits better.

---

## Template

```
Hi {FIRST NAME},

I'm Utkarsh with Teamcast. We are assembling a Customer Advisory Board of experienced leaders in the hiring and recruiting space. I {PERSONALIZED LINE} and thought we may have alignment.

Teamcast supports high growth teams by replacing resume driven screening with structured AI interviews and objective candidate scoring. We help recruiters focus only on the strongest applicants while improving consistency and speed across hiring teams.

This board will bring together a small group of leaders to share insight on hiring efficiency, candidate quality, and global scaling. Members have direct input to our roadmap, early access to product features, and private benchmarking insights.

{SOFT CTA LINE}

Best,

Utkarsh Wagh
Teamcast Inc
–
San Jose, CA

{OPT-OUT LINE}
```

---

## Variable Definitions

### {FIRST NAME}
- Prospect's first name from contact record

### {PERSONALIZED LINE}
- Source-backed observation, typically operational or scaling-focused
- Example: "saw your team recently expanded into EMEA and is managing high applicant volume across roles"

### {SOFT CTA LINE}
Rotate from these variants:
- "Would {DAY1} or {DAY2} work for a short introductory conversation?"
- "Happy to share more about what participation looks like. Would {DAY1} or {DAY2} work?"
- "I can send more details if helpful. Are you available {DAY1} or {DAY2}?"

### {DAY1} and {DAY2}
- Dynamic dates based on send day

### {OPT-OUT LINE}
Rotate from approved variants (see email-01-initial.md)

---

## When to Use This Template
- Signal is operational or scaling-based
- Prospect is in recruiting ops, TA management, or talent operations
- "High growth teams" framing fits the company better
- Company is clearly in rapid scaling mode
- Signal type is growth or recruiting_ops

## Key Differences from Email 01
- Opens with "Utkarsh with Teamcast" (slightly more formal)
- Uses "assembling" instead of "forming" (implies more progress)
- Emphasizes "high growth teams" and "consistency and speed across hiring teams"
- CAB value framing includes "global scaling" angle
- Better suited for operationally-minded prospects
