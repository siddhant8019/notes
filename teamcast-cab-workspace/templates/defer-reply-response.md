# Defer Reply Response Templates

> For Utkarsh to use when a prospect says "not now, maybe later."
> NOT auto-sent.

---

## Variant A — With Specific Timeframe Given
```
Completely understand, {FIRST NAME}. I'll circle back in {TIMEFRAME}. Best of luck with {THEIR STATED REASON IF GIVEN}.

Best,
Utkarsh
```

## Variant B — No Specific Timeframe
```
No problem at all, {FIRST NAME}. I'll follow up in a couple of months to see if the timing is better then.

Best,
Utkarsh
```

## Variant C — Mid-Reorg or Major Change
```
Makes total sense, {FIRST NAME}. Transitions like that take priority. I'll reach out again once things settle down on your end.

Best,
Utkarsh
```

---

## Handling Rules
- Log the defer date (convert relative to absolute: "next quarter" → specific month)
- Schedule re-engagement for the stated timeframe
- Do not follow up before the defer date
- Do not add to suppression list (this is a warm lead, just not now)
- Mark contact as "deferred" in campaign state
