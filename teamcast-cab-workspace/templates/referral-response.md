# Referral Response Templates

> For when a prospect points to someone else.
> Thank the referrer. Route the referral to Utkarsh for follow-up.

---

## Variant A — Thank Referrer
```
Appreciate you pointing me in the right direction, {FIRST NAME}. I'll reach out to {REFERRED NAME}. Thanks for your time.

Best,
Utkarsh
```

## Variant B — Thank and Acknowledge
```
That's helpful, {FIRST NAME} — thank you. I'll connect with {REFERRED NAME} directly. Much appreciated.

Best,
Utkarsh
```

---

## Outreach to Referred Contact

```
Hi {REFERRED FIRST NAME},

{REFERRER NAME} suggested I reach out to you. I'm Utkarsh from Teamcast — we're forming a Customer Advisory Board of experienced leaders in the hiring and recruiting space.

Teamcast helps hiring teams reduce resume noise and deliver interview-ready shortlists using structured AI-led assessments. We're inviting a select group of leaders to help shape the future of evidence-based hiring, with direct roadmap input, early access to features, and private benchmarking insights.

Would {DAY1} or {DAY2} work for a short introductory conversation?

Best,

Utkarsh Wagh
Teamcast Inc
–
San Jose, CA

If this is not relevant, let me know and I will stop here.
```

---

## Handling Rules
- Thank the referrer promptly
- Suppress the referrer from further CAB outreach (they've done their part)
- Route the referral to Utkarsh for personal outreach
- When emailing the referred contact, mention who referred them (social proof)
- The referred contact goes through normal QA before sending
- Log the referral chain in campaign data
