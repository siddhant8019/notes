# Not Interested Response Templates

> For when a prospect politely declines.
> Keep it gracious and brief. Add to suppression. Do not argue.

---

## Variant A — Standard
```
Appreciate you letting me know, {FIRST NAME}. I'll remove you from future outreach. Wishing you and the team well.

Best,
Utkarsh
```

## Variant B — Minimal
```
Understood, {FIRST NAME}. Thanks for letting me know. I'll step back.

Best,
Utkarsh
```

---

## Handling Rules
- Respond briefly and graciously (or don't respond at all — both are acceptable)
- Add contact to suppression list immediately
- Never argue, re-pitch, or ask "why not"
- Never follow up again
- Log the decline with any stated reason (useful for learning)
