# Email 01 — Initial Outreach

> Use when: Strong personalization signal, primary ICP, high company fit score.

---

## Template

```
Hi {FIRST NAME},

I'm Utkarsh from Teamcast. We're forming a Customer Advisory Board and reaching out to a small group of leaders in the hiring and recruiting space. I {PERSONALIZED LINE} and thought we may have alignment.

Teamcast helps hiring teams reduce resume noise and deliver interview ready shortlists using structured AI led assessments. Instead of manual screening, we evaluate skills, communication, and role fit before recruiters spend time on early calls.

We're inviting a select group of Heads of Talent and People leaders to help shape the future of evidence based hiring. Members have direct input to our roadmap, early access to product features, and private benchmarking insights.

{SOFT CTA LINE}

Best,

Utkarsh Wagh
Teamcast Inc
–
San Jose, CA

{OPT-OUT LINE}
```

---

## Variable Definitions

### {FIRST NAME}
- Prospect's first name from contact record

### {PERSONALIZED LINE}
- Source-backed observation about the prospect or their company
- Must start with a verb that follows "I" (noticed, saw, etc.)
- Example: "noticed you are scaling engineering across multiple regions and hiring aggressively this quarter"

### {SOFT CTA LINE}
Rotate from these variants (do not use "I am sure you may have questions"):
- "Happy to share more context if helpful. Are you available {DAY1} or {DAY2} for a brief conversation?"
- "Would {DAY1} or {DAY2} work for a short discussion?"
- "I can send more details ahead of time if useful. Would {DAY1} or {DAY2} work?"
- "Happy to share what participation looks like before we speak. Are you free {DAY1} or {DAY2}?"

### {DAY1} and {DAY2}
- Dynamic dates based on send day (see scheduling-rules.md)
- Must be actual business day names

### {OPT-OUT LINE}
Rotate from these variants:
- "If you're not open to board opportunities, just let me know and I will step back."
- "If this is not relevant, let me know and I will stop here."
- "If a CAB conversation is not of interest, I am happy to step back."
- "If this is not something you are exploring, just let me know."

---

## When to Use This Template
- Prospect has a strong personalization signal (confidence >= 7)
- Prospect is a primary ICP title
- Company fit score is high
- Signal type is hiring or expansion

## When NOT to Use
- Signal is weak or generic (use Email 03 instead)
- Prospect is a secondary/operational title (consider Email 03)
- No credible signal found (use generic fallback framing in Email 03)
