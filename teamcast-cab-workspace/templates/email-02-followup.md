# Email 02 — Follow-Up

> Use ONLY when: No reply, no bounce, no auto-reply, no unsubscribe after Email 01 or 03.
> Minimum 3 business days after initial send.

---

## Template

```
Hi {FIRST NAME},

Following up in case my note about the Teamcast Customer Advisory Board got buried.

We are confirming our final group of members and I would value your perspective. Would you have time {DAY1} or {DAY2} for a short discussion?

Best,

Utkarsh Wagh
Teamcast Inc
–
San Jose, CA

{OPT-OUT LINE}
```

---

## Variable Definitions

### {FIRST NAME}
- Same as original send

### {DAY1} and {DAY2}
- Dynamic dates based on follow-up send day
- Must be actual business day names

### {OPT-OUT LINE}
Rotate from approved variants (see email-01-initial.md)

---

## Eligibility Criteria (ALL must be true)
- [ ] Contact received Email 01 or Email 03
- [ ] No reply of any kind received
- [ ] No bounce recorded
- [ ] No auto-reply active (or return date has passed)
- [ ] No unsubscribe request
- [ ] Contact not on suppression list
- [ ] No internal owner conflict
- [ ] Minimum 3 business days since initial send
- [ ] Maximum 7 business days since initial send

## Rules
- Maximum ONE follow-up per contact per campaign
- Keep it short — this is a bump, not a re-pitch
- Do not re-introduce Teamcast or re-explain the CAB
- Do not add new personalization (the original email had it)
- Tone: polite, brief, respectful of their time
