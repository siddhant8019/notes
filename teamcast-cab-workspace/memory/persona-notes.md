## Primary Personas

### Head of Talent Acquisition / Director of TA
- **Day-to-day pain**: Managing high applicant volume, maintaining screening consistency across recruiters, balancing speed with quality
- **What they care about**: Tools that reduce noise, consistent evaluation frameworks, recruiter productivity
- **Messaging emphasis**: Screening consistency, recruiter bandwidth, applicant volume, shortlist quality
- **Tone**: Practitioner-level, operational, specific
- **Subject line preference**: Direct — "{FIRST NAME} board invite", "Advisory board invite"
- **CAB value angle**: Help shape a product that directly solves their daily screening burden

### VP of Recruiting / Director of Recruiting
- **Day-to-day pain**: Scaling hiring operations, managing recruiting team efficiency, reporting to leadership on hiring metrics
- **What they care about**: Operational leverage, team productivity, defensible hiring processes
- **Messaging emphasis**: Recruiter leverage, process efficiency, scaling hiring operations
- **Tone**: Strategic-operational, metrics-aware
- **Subject line preference**: Professional — "Advisory board invite", "{FIRST NAME} advisory seat"
- **CAB value angle**: Influence a product roadmap that could improve their team's operating model

### Chief People Officer / VP People
- **Day-to-day pain**: Organizational scaling, hiring quality at volume, building defensible people processes
- **What they care about**: Evidence-based decisions, consistency across geographies, strategic workforce planning
- **Messaging emphasis**: Hiring efficiency, evidence-based evaluation, process defensibility, scaling consistency
- **Tone**: Strategic, executive-level, outcome-focused
- **Subject line preference**: Elevated — "Private board invite", "{FIRST NAME} advisory invite"
- **CAB value angle**: Contribute strategic perspective to how AI transforms hiring at scale

## Secondary Personas

### COO
- **What they care about**: Operating efficiency, cost per hire, hiring velocity
- **Messaging emphasis**: Hiring velocity, recruiter leverage, signal quality, operating efficiency
- **Tone**: Business-outcome focused, concise
- **CAB value angle**: Operational insight into how hiring efficiency impacts company scaling

### CEO
- **What they care about**: Speed to hire, quality of talent pipeline, competitive advantage
- **Messaging emphasis**: Hiring velocity, competitive advantage, evidence-based decisions
- **Tone**: Strategic, high-level, brief
- **CAB value angle**: Shape how AI-powered hiring becomes a competitive moat

### CFO
- **What they care about**: Cost efficiency, recruiter ROI, predictable hiring spend
- **Messaging emphasis**: Cost per quality hire, recruiter productivity, budget efficiency
- **Tone**: Numbers-aware, outcome-focused
- **CAB value angle**: Insight into how evidence-based screening reduces wasted hiring spend

### VP HR / Director People Ops
- **What they care about**: Process consistency, compliance, employee experience from day one
- **Messaging emphasis**: Consistent evaluation, fair screening, process standardization
- **Tone**: Process-oriented, compliance-aware
- **CAB value angle**: Influence how AI-led assessments maintain fairness and consistency
