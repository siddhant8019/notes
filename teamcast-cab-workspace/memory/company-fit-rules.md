# Company Fit Rules

> Defines what makes a company a good fit for the Teamcast CAB campaign.
> Used by the Sourcing Agent to score and filter companies.

---

## Must-Have Criteria (All Required)

1. **Industry**: Technology, SaaS, Software Development, AI, FinTech, HealthTech, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, Enterprise SaaS, Tech-Enabled Services, BPO & Outsourcing, Product-Based Technology
2. **Headcount**: 0–500 (current batch)
3. **Geography**: North America, Europe, or APAC
4. **At least one growth signal**:
   - Recently raised funding (any round), OR
   - Employee headcount growth >= 15% (trailing 12 months)

## Scoring Model

| Factor | Weight | Scoring |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Industry match | 20% | Exact match = 10, Adjacent = 6, Weak = 2 |
| Headcount sweet spot | 20% | 50-200 = 10, 200-500 = 8, 0-50 = 5 |
| Recent funding | 20% | Yes (last 6 months) = 10, Yes (6-12 months) = 7, No = 0 |
| Headcount growth | 20% | >= 25% = 10, 15-25% = 8, 10-15% = 5, < 10% = 0 |
| Hiring activity | 20% | 10+ open roles = 10, 5-10 = 7, 1-5 = 4, 0 = 0 |

**Minimum fit score to proceed**: 6/10 weighted average

## Strong Fit Signals
- Startup that just raised Series A/B and is building product/engineering team
- Company with 15%+ growth that recently posted multiple TA/recruiting roles
- Tech company expanding into new geographies with hiring needs
- Company that recently hired their first Head of TA (scaling inflection point)

## Weak Fit Signals (Proceed with Caution)
- Large enterprise (500+) — likely has established processes
- Pre-seed/angel stage — may not have structured hiring needs yet
- Non-tech industry even if growing — may not value AI-led screening
- Company with no visible hiring activity despite growth metrics

## Exclusions
- Companies with < 5 employees (too early)
- Companies in industries unrelated to technology
- Companies headquartered in regions outside NA/Europe/APAC
- Known Teamcast customers (check CRM)
- Known competitors to Teamcast
