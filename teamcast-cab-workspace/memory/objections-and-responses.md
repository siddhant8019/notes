# Objections and Responses

> Common objections from CAB outreach replies and recommended handling.
> Updated by the Replies Agent and Learning Agent.

---

## Objection Categories

### "Not interested"
**Typical phrasing**: "No thanks", "Not interested", "Please remove me"
**Response**: Respect immediately. Add to suppression. No follow-up.
**Note**: This is a clean exit — do not try to re-engage.

### "What is this exactly?"
**Typical phrasing**: "Can you tell me more?", "What does participation involve?"
**Response**: Route to Utkarsh. This is a warm lead. Send details about CAB structure, time commitment, and benefits.
**Classify as**: positive

### "I'm not the right person"
**Typical phrasing**: "You should talk to [Name]", "I don't handle recruiting"
**Response**: Thank them. Log the referral. Route referral to Utkarsh. Suppress original contact.
**Classify as**: referral or not_relevant

### "Not right now / Maybe later"
**Typical phrasing**: "Reach out next quarter", "We're mid-reorg", "Timing isn't right"
**Response**: Log defer date. Schedule re-engagement for the specified time. No follow-up now.
**Classify as**: defer

### "We already have a solution"
**Typical phrasing**: "We use [competitor]", "We have this covered"
**Response**: Note the competitive intelligence. Suppress unless they express curiosity. Do not argue or compare.
**Classify as**: no_interest

### "How did you get my email?"
**Typical phrasing**: "Where did you find me?", "How did you get my contact info?"
**Response**: Escalate to human. Be transparent about sourcing (publicly available business contact information). Offer to remove immediately.
**Classify as**: escalate

### Hostile / Spam Complaint
**Typical phrasing**: "This is spam", "I'll report this", "Stop emailing me"
**Response**: Suppress immediately. Add domain to watch list. Alert operator. Do not respond.
**Classify as**: hostile

## Response Templates (For Utkarsh to Use)

The Teamcast Customer Advisory Board is a small group of experienced hiring and recruiting leaders who meet periodically to share perspectives on hiring efficiency, candidate quality, and scaling. Members get direct input to our product roadmap, early access to features, and private benchmarking insights. Happy to share more on a brief call — would [day] or [day] work?

> Appreciate you pointing me in the right direction, [First Name]. I'll reach out to [Referred Name]. Thanks for your time.

### To "Maybe later"
> Completely understand, [First Name]. I'll circle back in [timeframe]. Best of luck with [their stated reason if given].
