## Teamcast Context

- **What Teamcast does**: Helps hiring teams reduce resume noise and deliver interview-ready shortlists using structured AI-led assessments. Instead of manual screening, Teamcast evaluates skills, communication, and role fit before recruiters spend time on early calls.
- **Core value proposition**: Delivers fully vetted, interview-ready Top 3 candidates
- **Positioning**: Not generic AI hiring — an evidence-based engine that replaces resume-driven screening with structured AI interviews and objective candidate scoring
- **Target market**: High-growth tech teams scaling hiring operations
